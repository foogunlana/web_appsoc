Welcome to AppSoc, Please register your email in the 'Sign Up' tab to join our mailing list! We will keep you updated on meetings and events


    What's AppSoc?

    App Society, formerly known as Mobile Applications Society, is a community of bright minds, hackers, and innovators based in Imperial College, London. We're devoted to sharing our knowledge of programming to anyone interested. Visit our About Us page for more information.

    What we do

    AppSoc teaches students to make apps through fun lessons, and exciting competitions. Our members get free lessons in iOS and Android programming. Visit our Learn page for more information on our lessons.

    Where to find us

    You can find us on campus at Imperial College. We will let you know our times and venues through the mailing list so please Sign Up. To contact us directly visit the Contact Us page.
