Our Story


"To give other students the tools to make a positive impact on the world of technology ... And to have fun while doing it!"

About AppSoc

AppSoc is a community of creative thinkers who want to share their ideas with the world. You don't even need to know any programming to join, we'll teach it to you. We have members of various strengths and abilities because AppSoc was founded to bring together the various disciplines of Imperial College to think as one. Two heads are better than one, especially when you're trying to solve the world's problems using mobile applications! No pressure...

Our Motivation

What better way is there to change the world than to change the minds in it? We are joining the movement to rethink education from the inside out. We want to prove to you that you don't need a Computer Science degree to code. We know that there are so many students just sitting on amazing ideas that they can't build. We're here to teach them how. And besides... Apps are fun
